CognitionBase is a spinoff de innovación of 23people.io. It operates as an MVP/product incubator with radical speed as a core value.

**Services:**

- Idea2Market Service: transforms business ideas into functional MVPs/demos/landing pages
- Technical Evaluations Service: technical assessment of external product ideas

**Team structure (as of 2026-04-20):**

- Lead Dev AI: exercises both Technical Lead role and SDM (Service Delivery Manager) role
- SRM (Service Request Manager): manages client requests, validates them before they enter the delivery flow
- Devs AI: human developers + AI agents collectively called Iron-Legion
