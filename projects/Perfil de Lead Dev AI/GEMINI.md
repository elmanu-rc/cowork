# GEMINI.md - CognitionBase: Lead Dev AI Profile & Strategy

This directory contains the foundational documentation, strategic vision, and reference materials for the **Lead Dev AI (The Architect)** position at **CognitionBase**, an innovation spinoff of [23people.io](https://23people.io).

## Directory Overview

The purpose of this workspace is to define the role, responsibilities, and operational framework (the "Iron-Legion" protocol) for a Lead Dev AI who will spearhead the creation of digital products using an AI-First methodology.

## Key Files

- **`docs/LEAD-DEV-AI-JOBPOSITION.md`**: The primary job description. It outlines the "Architect" role, core functions (Blueprinting, AI-First leadership), and technical requirements.
- **`docs/references/COGNITIONBASE.md`**: The strategic vision for the incubator. It defines the **Iron-Legion Protocol**, mission, and workflow (from Idea to MVP in < 6 weeks).
- **`docs/references/GSD-README.md`**: Documentation for the **Get Shit Done (GSD)** system, a meta-prompting and context engineering framework that informs the project's execution philosophy.
- **`docs/REFERENCES.md`**: A collection of external links and internal resources used to build this profile.

## Operational Framework: The Iron-Legion

CognitionBase operates under a radical "AI-First" and "Output-First" mentality:

1. **Blueprinting**: The Lead Dev AI transforms business ideas into technical master plans.
2. **Iron-Legion**: A hybrid team of human AI Developers and AI Agents (A/Gentes).
3. **Tech Stack**: Prioritizes "Boring Tools for High Speed" (Next.js, Tailwind, Supabase, Vercel).
4. **GSD-Inspired**: Focused on extreme ownership, atomic decomposition of tasks, and rapid delivery cycles (Landing Page in 7-10 days, MVP in < 6 weeks).

## Usage Instructions

Use the contents of this directory to:

- Draft or refine job postings for Lead Dev AI positions.
- Understand the technical and philosophical standards of the Iron-Legion protocol.
- Generate "Blueprints" or technical strategies aligned with the CognitionBase mission.
- Onboard new members to the AI-First development workflow.
