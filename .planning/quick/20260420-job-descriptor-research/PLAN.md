# Research: Framework para Descriptores de Cargo Formales

Research canonical framework for formal job descriptors in tech startups, covering:
1. Canonical structure of a formal job descriptor (required sections)
2. Conceptual distinction: Job/Position vs Role vs Function vs Responsibility
3. Modeling a position with multiple roles (Lead Dev AI + SDM)
4. Seniority levels (Junior/Mid/Senior tracks) within a single position
5. Terminology: "Job Position" vs "Role" vs "Cargo"
6. Modern frameworks (Radford/Mercer, RACI, competency frameworks) for small AI-first startups
7. Pragmatic approach for small AI-first companies
